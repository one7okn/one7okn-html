Welcome to one7okn

one7okn's mission is to craft institutional-grade ETV offering exposures and hedging solutions to the NFT Art space by leveraging Quantitative DeFi.

one7okn is building the Diagora Protocol as a series of NFT-themed vaults that are built on top of existing NFT financialization primitives

Our first product is an NFT Floor Price Option Automated Market Maker, deployed by Diagora Protocol. We are initially launching on Mainnet.
